Flower

Rose

Roses are the masterpiece of all the flowers as it emits beautiful positive feelings.It is traditionally used for romance and weddings. Red roses are a symbol of love. White roses are symbol of innocence, purity and reverence.

Water-Lily

Water lily is an aquatic plant that is most easily recognized by its eye-catching, open bloom and uniquely-shaped leaves.The waterlilies perfectly symbolise innocence, purity, pleasure, celebration, hope, rebirth, and peace.

Marigold

Marigold is a fragrant flower that grows easily everywhere and it is used in home decoration. Marigolds are the most potent source of these two vital Vision Detoxifier compounds(Lutein and Zeaxanthin).

Sunflower

The sunflower has many meanings across the world. Different cultures believe it means anything from positivity and strength to admiration and loyalty. Sunflower seeds are a source of many vitamins and minerals.
